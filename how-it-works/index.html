    FAQ

    Common Questions

    Here's what most people ask before getting started.

    Do I need to replace my current software?

    Not necessarily. We can usually build the automation around the tools you already use.

    Can a small business benefit from automation?

    Yes. Automating even one repetitive, time-consuming process can make a meaningful difference.

    Will my team stay in control?

    Yes. Automation handles the steps you choose, while your team keeps control of decisions, exceptions, and customer relationships.

    What processes can be automated?

    Most repetitive, rules-based processes. Tell us what you want handled, and we'll confirm what's possible.

    How long does implementation take?

    It depends on the tools involved and the complexity of your request. You'll get a clear project scope before development begins.

    Will you show us how it works?

    Yes. We'll walk your team through the finished system so everyone knows how to use it.
