    How it works

    This automation installs a tawk.to live chat widget directly on your website, then configures its AI Assist feature to automatically answer visitor questions using your website content and any help articles or documents you provide. Visitors get instant answers to common questions (hours, pricing, services, location) without waiting for a human.

    If a question is outside what the bot knows, it hands the conversation to a real agent if one's online, or captures the message so you can follow up. You keep full visibility into every conversation, and the bot keeps learning as you add more content to its knowledge base.

    ⚠️ A quick note on cost

    tawk.to's core live chat widget is free forever, unlimited agents, unlimited chats, unlimited websites. The automated AI replies are a paid add-on called AI Assist, which starts at a free "Hobby" tier (100 AI replies/month) and moves to $29/month for 1,000 AI replies/month at higher volume.

    For most small businesses, the free AI tier is enough to test the waters; if your chat volume grows past that, the $29/month tier is the next step up. Either way, this cost is billed directly by tawk.to, separate from our investment above. Check tawk.to's pricing page for current numbers.

    ✅ No developer needed on your end

    We handle the widget install, branding, and AI training. You'll just need to give us access to add a small snippet to your website (or we can do it directly if we're managing your site).
