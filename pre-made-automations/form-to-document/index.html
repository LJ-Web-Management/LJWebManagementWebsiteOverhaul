    Form Response to Filled Document

    Form Gets Submitted

    A client or team member fills out a Microsoft Forms form, a quote request, work order, or intake form.

    📄

    Template Gets Filled

    Power Automate drops the submitted answers into a Word template, then converts it to a polished PDF.

    📤

    Document Gets Sent

    The finished PDF is automatically emailed to the requester, the client, or your internal team.

    How it works

    Once set up, this automation watches for new responses to a Microsoft Form you choose. When someone submits it, Power Automate takes the answers and fills them into a Word template you provide (a quote sheet, work order, offer letter, whatever your business sends out repeatedly), then converts the finished document to PDF.

    From there, it emails the PDF to whoever should receive it, the client, the requester, or your own team, so a document that used to take someone 10-15 minutes to build by hand now takes zero.

    Built On

    Microsoft connectors that work together to generate your documents:

    Microsoft Forms
    Word Online
    Outlook

    ⚠️ This one needs a Power Automate Premium license

    Unlike most of our Microsoft automations, this one relies on the "Populate a Microsoft Word template" action, which Microsoft classifies as a Premium connector (Word Online Business). That means it does not run on a standard Microsoft 365 plan alone.

    You'll need a Power Automate Premium license (roughly $15/user/month at time of writing, billed by Microsoft, separate from our build fee) for whichever account owns the flow. We'll confirm your current license status before starting the build, so there are no surprises, and flag if an upgrade is needed.

    ⚠️ A note on your Word template

    You'll need a Word template with the fields you want auto-filled marked as content controls (we can help set this up, or build it from scratch based on a document you're already using). Rich text formatting inside certain fields and very large templates have some known limitations with this connector, we'll flag anything relevant to your specific template during setup.
