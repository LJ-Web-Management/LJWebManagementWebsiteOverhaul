    How it works

    This agent runs on xAI's Grok Voice Agent, a real-time, speech-to-speech AI built specifically for phone conversations. It's set up with your specific qualifying criteria, budget, timeline, need, whatever matters most for your business, and works those questions naturally into the conversation.

    By the end of the call, you know whether the lead is worth pursuing, and your team gets a clear summary instead of a cold callback list.
