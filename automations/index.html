    Real Estate

    5 automations
    Real Estate Agent / Broker

    Listing-to-Everywhere Syndicator

    The moment a new listing goes live, this automation pulls the photos, price, and description and pushes them out to the agent's Facebook and Instagram business pages, a branded email blast to the agent's buyer list, and a text alert to any client whose saved search matches the property. It also generates a single-property landing page automatically, so the agent never has to manually re-type listing details across five different platforms again.

    Tools Required
    Zapier or Make.comMLS/IDX data feed (e.g., Bridge Interactive or Spark API)Meta Business Suite (Facebook/Instagram)Mailchimp or ActiveCampaignTwilio (SMS)A simple landing page builder (e.g., Carrd or Unbounce)
    Property Manager

    Maintenance Request Triage & Dispatch

    Tenants submit maintenance issues through a simple online form or text line, and the system automatically classifies the urgency (emergency, urgent, routine), notifies the correct vendor or in-house technician, opens a work order, and keeps the tenant updated on status via automated texts, all without the property manager touching a spreadsheet or playing phone tag with contractors.

    Tools Required
    Jotform or Typeform (intake form)Zapier or Make.comTwilio (SMS updates)A property management platform (e.g., Buildium or AppFolio)Slack or Microsoft Teams (internal alerts)
    Real Estate Marketing Manager

    Open House Lead Nurture Sequence

    Every visitor who signs in at an open house (via QR code or tablet) is automatically added to a nurture sequence: an immediate thank-you text with a link to the listing, a follow-up email with similar properties two days later, and an automatic hand-off to the agent's CRM with a lead-score tag so hot leads get a phone call instead of another email.

    Tools Required
    A digital sign-in tool (e.g., Google Forms or SpotOn)Zapier or Make.comCRM (e.g., Follow Up Boss or KVCore)Mailchimp or ActiveCampaignTwilio (SMS)
    Transaction Coordinator

    Closing Document Tracker & Deadline Reminder

    This automation reads a transaction's key dates (inspection period, financing deadline, closing date) from the contract or CRM, builds a shared timeline automatically, and sends escalating reminders to the agent, buyer, seller, and lender as deadlines approach, plus a daily digest to the transaction coordinator showing exactly which files are missing a signed document.

    Tools Required
    DocuSign or DotloopZapier or Make.comGoogle Sheets or AirtableTwilio or email (reminders)CRM (e.g., Follow Up Boss)
    Leasing Agent

    Lead Qualification & Tour Scheduler

    Rental inquiries coming in from Zillow, Apartments.com, or the property website are automatically qualified against move-in date, budget, and pet policy, then routed straight into the leasing agent's calendar for a tour, no more back-and-forth texting to find a time that works. Unqualified leads get a polite automated response instead of silence.

    Tools Required
    Zapier or Make.comCalendlyTwilio (SMS)A property listing syndication tool (e.g., Zillow Rental Manager API)CRM or Google Sheets

    Tools Required

    Staff Shift Scheduling & Coverage Alert System

    Tools Required
    Scheduling software (e.g., When I Work or ShiftKey)Zapier or Make.comTwilio (SMS)Slack or Teams (manager alerts)
