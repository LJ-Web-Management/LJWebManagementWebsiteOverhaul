    Cannabis & Hemp Automations

    5 done-for-you automations built for the roles that keep this industry running. Book a consultation and we'll tailor any of these to your exact tools.

    Book a Consultation See How It Works
    Dispensary Owner

    Loyalty Program & High-Value Customer Re-Engagement System

    Every purchase automatically updates the customer's loyalty point balance, and customers who hit a tier milestone receive an automatic notification with their reward offer and an expiration date that creates urgency without being pushy. Customers who haven't purchased in 30 days enter a re-engagement sequence, a "we miss you" message with a limited offer on a product category they've purchased before. VIP customers identified by spend threshold receive early access announcements for new drops and limited menu items, giving the dispensary a direct revenue lever that doesn't depend on paid advertising every time a new product arrives.

    Tools Required
    Dispensary POS (e.g., Dutchie, Treez, or Jane Technologies) with loyalty moduleZapier or Make.comTwilio (loyalty milestone and re-engagement SMS, compliant channels only)Mailchimp or email (VIP and lapsed customer sequences)Airtable (customer segment tracker)
    Compliance Manager

    State Reporting & Seed-to-Sale Track-and-Trace Automation

    Every inventory movement (receiving, transfer, sale, destruction, or adjustment) is logged in real time and automatically synced to the state's required track-and-trace system (METRC, BioTrack, or equivalent) so that the compliance manager's reporting obligation is met continuously rather than in a stressful end-of-month data dump. Any inventory discrepancy identified between the POS and the track-and-trace system triggers an immediate compliance alert, and the compliance manager receives a daily reconciliation summary confirming that all movement is accounted for before the next state inspection catches what the internal process missed.

    Tools Required
    Dispensary or cultivation software with METRC/BioTrack integration (e.g., Leaf Trade or COVA)Zapier or Make.comAirtable (discrepancy log and compliance tracker)Slack or email (daily reconciliation summary and discrepancy alerts)
    Cannabis Cultivator

    Environmental Monitoring Alert & Grow Log System

    Sensors in each grow room continuously monitor temperature, humidity, CO2, and light schedules, and any reading outside the set parameters triggers an immediate alert to the head grower and cultivation manager via text, because a HVAC failure that goes unnoticed for six hours during a critical flowering stage can destroy an entire room's yield. Throughout the grow cycle, the cultivation team logs daily observations via mobile form (plant height, leaf color, pest sightings, watering notes) and the system builds a complete grow log by room, strain, and batch that is available for compliance inspection and internal quality review without anyone compiling it from handwritten notes after the fact.

    Tools Required
    Environmental sensors with API output (e.g., Trolmaster or Growlink)Zapier or Make.comJotform (daily grow observation log)Twilio (out-of-range environmental alerts)Airtable (grow log by room and batch)
    Cannabis Wholesale Sales Rep

    B2B Dispensary Account Reorder & Pricing Update System

    Each week, this automation checks every dispensary account's order history, identifies accounts that are typically on a weekly or bi-weekly reorder cycle and haven't placed a new order, and sends the account buyer a personalized reorder reminder with their standard product list and current pricing pre-populated in a simple order form. When new products hit the menu or pricing is updated, all active accounts receive an automatic product sheet or price list update from their account rep, so the buyer is informed before they order from a competitor simply because they didn't know the product existed. The sales rep spends time on relationships and new accounts rather than manually chasing reorders.

    Tools Required
    CRM (e.g., HubSpot or a cannabis B2B platform like Leaf Trade)Zapier or Make.comJotform (digital order form)Mailchimp or email (product announcements and reorder reminders)Airtable (account order cycle tracker)
    Dispensary Budtender Manager

    Staff Training Compliance & Product Knowledge Quiz System

    When new products are added to the menu, this automation sends every budtender a product education brief and a short quiz to confirm they understand the effects, recommended use cases, and any relevant compliance disclosures before they sell it to a customer. Quiz results are logged per employee, and budtenders who score below the threshold receive the material again with a re-test prompt before they are cleared to sell the product. The budtender manager sees a real-time training compliance dashboard showing who has completed required trainings and who has outstanding items, keeping customer service quality and compliance standards consistent across every shift.

    Tools Required
    Google Forms or Jotform (product quiz)Zapier or Make.comGoogle Sheets (training compliance tracker)Slack (new product training announcement)Email (quiz result notification and remediation prompts)
